Last Updated: April 26, 2026

Welcome to So Young. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by So Young and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

So Young is designed as an offline-first application. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use the core features of the app.

  • Local Data: Your "Favorite" names and search history are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal information about the device you use to access the app, such as the model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for crash reporting and performance optimization.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: For app functionality and updates.
  • AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your advertising ID to provide personalized ads.
  • Firebase / Google Analytics: We use these tools to understand app usage patterns and improve user experience. This data is anonymized.

3. Security

We value your trust in providing us your non-personal information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

4. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
